About

I graduated from Oxford University with First Class Honours in English in 2017. For the next few years, I worked as editor of the Oxford University Press education website Oxford Owl.

Last year, I moved to New York to pursue M.S. Data Journalism at Columbia University. I am now joining San Francisco's Mission Local to do reporting and data viz.

I have a particular interest in environmental news and building fun data tools. In my spare time, I love hiking, drawing, and writing web bios.
